Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I visit the dentist?

For optimal oral health, we recommend visiting your dentist for a routine check-up and professional cleaning every six months. This allows us to detect and address potential issues early, preventing more serious problems down the line.

What are dental implants?

Dental implants are permanent solutions for missing teeth. They consist of a small titanium post that is surgically placed into the jawbone, acting as an artificial tooth root. A crown, bridge, or denture is then attached to this post, providing a stable, natural-looking replacement for missing teeth.

Is root canal treatment painful?

Contrary to popular belief, modern root canal treatment is usually no more uncomfortable than a regular filling thanks to advanced techniques and effective local anesthesia. The procedure is designed to relieve the pain caused by an infected tooth, not cause it.

Do you offer pediatric dental services?

Yes, we do! We offer specialized, friendly dental care for children of all ages. Our team is dedicated to creating positive dental experiences, focusing on prevention, education, and teaching good oral hygiene habits from a young age.

What payment options are available?
